08 — Common Questions
What We Hear Most Often
How does this compare to Teladoc?
Teladoc covers urgent care only. Amaze Health includes primary care, urgent care, mental health, dermatology, patient advocacy, and hospital indemnity — all in one platform.
Can we offer this to everyone?
Yes — any W-2 employee who earns enough income to be positively impacted can participate. Typically offered to full-time employees, though part-time and seasonal W-2 workers may also qualify.
What happens during unpaid leave?
If an employee goes on unpaid leave, the benefit pauses automatically. No paycheck means no deduction — it resumes when they return to active payroll.
What's the workload for our payroll team?
There is some initial setup — adding two new line items (BMPR & BMCL) to payroll and testing the first run. We guide your team through every step. Once live, ongoing maintenance is minimal.
How do we get employees to enroll?
Positively impacted employees would be auto-enrolled — they'd get a bigger paycheck plus benefits. We provide all communications, meeting materials, and a dedicated enrollment team.
What does communication look like?
We build a custom communication plan — branded emails, flyers, and employee meeting presentations. Your team approves everything before it goes out.
