08 — The Challenge
Healthcare today is broken
for working families.
Cost
Premiums, deductibles, and out-of-pocket costs keep rising — pricing families out of care.
Connection
Finding a primary care doctor is harder than ever. Wait times are weeks — especially in northern Michigan.
Confusion
Employees don't understand their benefits, can't read an EOB, and don't know where to turn.
Convenience
Taking time off work to sit in a waiting room isn't realistic — especially for employees keeping operations running.
For employees already stretched by rising costs and high deductibles, these problems make healthcare feel out of reach — even when they have coverage.
