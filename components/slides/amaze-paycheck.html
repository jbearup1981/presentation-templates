How This Works
BMPR — Pre-tax premium deduction. Lowers taxable income before taxes are calculated.

BMCL — Claims payment (preventative education benefit). Added back after taxes. Lower than the premium, but because taxes dropped significantly, the net result is positive.
Important: BMPR and BMCL are two separate line items. One lowers taxable income, the other is a claims payment.
