What This Means
If an employee on Plan 1500 is hospitalized for 3 days, they receive $3,400 in cash — $2,500 admission + $900 confinement. Paid directly to the employee.
Monthly Claims Payment
Enrolled employees would receive $1,200/mo (Plan 1500) or $1,000/mo (Plan 1250) for completing a brief health education activity. This creates the paycheck math we'll show next.
