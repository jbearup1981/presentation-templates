03 — The Care Model
How Amaze Health Works
This isn't a telemedicine app with gig doctors. Amaze Health employs full-time, salaried medical providers who build ongoing relationships with patients across every specialty.
1. Reach Out
Message or call through the app. No phone trees, no intake forms, no waiting rooms.
2. See a Provider
Connected to a provider in under 30 seconds. They diagnose, treat, prescribe, and order labs.
3. Get Coordinated Care
Need a specialist? The care team coordinates referrals, follows up, and advocates on your behalf.
Full-Time Providers
Not contract or gig workers. Amaze Health doctors are salaried, W-2 employees who are invested in patient outcomes — not visit volume.
Care Coordination
One team manages everything — primary care, specialists, billing questions, prescriptions. Employees don't navigate the system alone.
